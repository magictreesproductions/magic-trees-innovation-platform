## IF TROUBLE COMES

### Scenario: Cease & Desist Letter

**Steps**:
1. Don't panic
2. Immediately stop alleged activity
3. Consult lawyer (real lawyer, not AI)
4. Preserve all evidence
5. Respond in writing (through lawyer)
6. Fix the issue
7. Make it public (transparency)
8. Update practices

### Scenario: Copyright Claim

**Steps**:
1. Verify claim (is it real?)
2. Check if we actually infringed
3. If yes: remove immediately, apologize, learn
4. If no: respond with evidence
5. Work toward resolution
6. Document resolution
7. Update processes to prevent recurrence

### Scenario: Tax/Legal Inquiry

**Steps**:
1. Cooperate fully
2. Provide documents requested
3. Consult tax professional
4. Answer honestly
5. Pay any owed amounts
6. Update compliance processes
7. Maintain clean records going forward
